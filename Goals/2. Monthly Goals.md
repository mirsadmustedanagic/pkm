## 🎯 Monthly Goals

### 🔴 Must Complete (Non-negotiable)
- [ ] **Establish stress management practice** — Pick one and do it 3x/week
  - Options to test: meditation (10min), journaling (15min), walking meditation, yoga
  - Success criteria: Have chosen a practice and logged 10+ sessions by July 31
  - Due: End of week 1, then consistent for month
  
- [ ] **Block 3h/week for deep work** — Put it on calendar and protect it
  - Success criteria: 3h protected time block visible on calendar; communicated to team
  - Due: End of week 1
  
- [ ] **Test meaningful work** — Identify 2-3 project types that could engage you
  - Success criteria: Have at least 2 different project experiments; written reflection on what felt different
  - Due: Mid-month check-in
  - Note: This can be a side project, learning goal, or work reframing—whatever you can test in 3h/week

### 🟡 Should Complete (Important but flexible)
- [ ] **Reach out to close friends** — Start rebuilding friendships
  - Action: Contact 2-3 people and suggest getting together in August
  - Why: Starts the relationship re-investment without requiring time this month yet

- [ ] **Start daily logging** — Create a daily note each day (or 5/7 days minimum)
  - Why: Foundation for the whole system; gives you data for weekly reviews
  - Don't overthink it—just daily template with focus + end-of-day reflection

### 🟢 Nice to Complete (Bonus)
- [ ] **Experiment with one creative outlet** — Just try it once
  - Anything: sketching, writing, building, designing, etc.
  - Goal: See if it sparks anything
