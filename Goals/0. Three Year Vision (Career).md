# Three Year Vision — Career (2026–2029)

## 🌟 Vision Statement

**In three years, I will have:**
- Clarity on what meaningful engineering work looks like for me and consistent access to it
- A protected deep work capacity of 3+ hours/week, sustained across quarters
- A systematized decision & learning log that compounds into visible technical depth
- Recognized ownership on netilion2 (and successor projects) with clear technical impact

**The shift:** From fragmented focus, reactive tasks, and unclear priorities → to protected deep work, clear daily focus, and systematic learning capture.

---

## 🎯 Focus Areas

### 💼 Meaningful Work Clarity
- [ ] **Identify meaningful work** — Define the problem shapes, project types, and impact areas that energize you
  - *Currently:* Feeling stuck in disorganized, unclear priorities
  - *Target:* Can articulate "this is the work I want more of" with concrete examples
- [ ] **Reduce busywork** — Cut low-impact tasks so meaningful work dominates
  - *Target:* 60%+ of work time on meaningful projects vs. operations

### 🧠 Deep Work Capacity
- [ ] **Protect deep work time** — Establish and defend a 3h/week minimum
  - *Currently:* No dedicated deep work time
  - *Target:* 3h/week protected on calendar with 80%+ adherence
- [ ] **Reduce context-switching** — Single-project focus per day where possible
  - *Target:* Morning note names ONE primary project each workday

### 📚 Systematic Learning Capture
- [ ] **Decision log habit** — Capture architectural decisions and their tradeoffs
  - *Target:* 20+ decisions logged per year across active projects
- [ ] **Technical mastery in one domain** — Deep expertise where it compounds
  - *Target:* Can lead design & mentor others in a specific technical area

### 🚀 Project Leadership
- [ ] **Own meaningful features end-to-end** on netilion2
  - *Target:* 2+ major features/initiatives led to completion per year
- [ ] **Reliable maintainer stance** on netilion-monorepo
  - *Target:* Predictable maintenance cadence, no crisis mode

---

## 📊 Success Metrics

### Year 1 (2026) — Foundation
- Daily morning + afternoon review ritual established
- 20+ decisions captured across projects
- 50–60% deep work maintained across the year
- Clear articulation of "meaningful work" for you

### Year 2 (2027) — Depth
- Deep technical mastery in one identified domain
- Recognized project leadership on netilion2
- Sustainable weekly rhythm with no burnout dips

### Year 3 (2028–2029) — Impact
- Track record of shipped meaningful work
- Influence on technical direction of primary project(s)
- Optional: mentorship or leadership expansion, chosen deliberately

## 💭 Reflection Questions

*Review quarterly:*

1. Does "meaningful work" mean the same thing today as three months ago?
2. Where did I lose deep work time this quarter — and to what?
3. What decisions from the log turned out to matter most?
4. What am I becoming an expert in?
5. Is netilion2 still the right primary project?
