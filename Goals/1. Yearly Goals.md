## 🎯 Annual Goals by Life Area

### 💼 Career & Professional (30% of effort — 3h/week from work)
- [ ] **Clarify meaningful work** — Test and identify what energizes you professionally
  - Q3: Identify 2-3 project types that feel meaningful; discuss with mentor/peer
  - Q4: Deep work on chosen direction; document what works and doesn't
  - Success metric: Clear answer to "What type of work energizes me?"
  
- [ ] **Protect deep work time** — Establish 3h/week rhythm
  - Q3: Block 3h/week on calendar; communicate boundaries to team
  - Q4: Achieve 80%+ adherence to deep work blocks
  - Success metric: 3h/week of uninterrupted focused work on high-impact projects

### 🏃 Health & Wellness (20% of effort)
- [ ] **Stress management practice** — Build daily/weekly mental clarity habit
  - Practice: Choose one (meditation, journaling, walking, yoga, etc.)
  - Target: 3x/week minimum
  - Success metric: Feel noticeably less overwhelmed by end of Q3
  
- [ ] **Maintain exercise routine** — Keep current habits consistent
  - Soccer: 1x/week (already doing)
  - Run/bike: 1x/week (already doing)
  - Bodyweight: daily, scattered (already doing)
  - Success metric: Logged 80% of weeks

### ❤️ Relationships (20% of effort)
- [ ] **Invest in friendships** — Regular meaningful time with people you care about
  - Q3: Reach out to 2-3 close friends; schedule first gathering
  - Q4: At least 1 monthly gathering with friends (dinner, activity, etc.)
  - Success metric: Feel connected and less isolated

- [ ] **Build support network** — Have people you can be vulnerable with
  - Q3: Identify 3-5 people you want to deepen connection with
  - Q4: Have real conversations with at least 3 of them
  - Success metric: Feel like you have people who know and support you

### 🌱 Personal Growth & Creativity (20% of effort — part of 5-7h/week personal time)
- [ ] **Rediscover creative outlets** — Find what brings you joy
  - Q3: Experiment with 3 creative activities (design, writing, building, art, etc.)
  - Q4: Choose one to practice regularly
  - Success metric: Have a creative practice you do at least 1x/week by year-end
  
- [ ] **Deep work on meaningful projects** — Experience flow and engagement
  - Q3-Q4: Apply "deep work" to a creative/personal project (not just career)
  - Success metric: Complete one small creative project that feels meaningful to you

### 💰 Financial & Practical (10% of effort)
- [ ] **Stabilize and protect time** — Reduce overcommitment
  - Q3: Audit time commitments; identify what to say NO to
  - Q4: Implement at least 2 boundaries (decline meetings, delegate tasks, etc.)
  - Success metric: Feel less overextended
