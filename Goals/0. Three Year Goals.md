# Three Year Goals (2024-2027)

## 🌟 Vision Statement

**In three years, I will have:**
- Regained clarity on my meaningful work and have deep focus time for impactful projects
- Built sustainable habits around mental health and stress management so I'm not constantly overextended
- Reconnected with friends and prioritized quality relationships
- Rediscovered creative outlets that energize me (beyond just work)

**The shift:** From giving too much and receiving too little → to protecting my time and energy, knowing what truly matters

---

## 🎯 Key Life Areas

### 💼 Career & Professional Development

- [ ] **Clarify meaningful work** — Discover/define the type of problem or impact that energizes you
  - *Currently: Feeling stuck in disorganized, meaningless tasks*
  - *Target: Have 3h/week of deep work on something that matters*
- [ ] **Build deep work capacity** — Protect and use focused time effectively
  - *Currently: No dedicated deep work time*
  - *Target: Consistent 3h/week on high-impact projects*
- [ ] **Escape busy work trap** — Reduce time on low-impact tasks
  - *Currently: Overwhelmed by meetings and admin*
  - *Target: 70% of work time on meaningful projects vs 30% on operations*

### 🏃 Health & Wellness

- [ ] **Mental clarity & stress management** — Move from overwhelm to calm
  - *Currently: No stress management practices, feeling overextended*
  - *Target: Regular practice (meditation, journaling, or similar) that creates mental space*
- [ ] **Maintain exercise momentum** — Keep current habits (soccer 1x/week, run/bike 1x/week, daily bodyweight)
  - *Currently: Active but scattered*
  - *Target: Consistent, intentional approach to fitness*
- [ ] **Reclaim personal time** — Protect at least 5-7h/week for non-work activities
  - *Currently: No protected personal time*
  - *Target: Regular blocks for creative work, friends, rest*

### ❤️ Relationships & Family

- [ ] **Invest in friendships** — Rebuild time with people you care about
  - *Currently: Neglected due to work overcommitment*
  - *Target: Regular meaningful time with friends (soccer provides some, but need more depth)*
- [ ] **Rebuild support network** — Have people you can rely on and who rely on you
  - *Currently: Isolated by being overextended*
  - *Target: Consistent presence and vulnerability with close people*

### 🌱 Personal Growth & Learning

- [ ] **Rediscover creativity** — Find outlets that bring joy and self-expression
  - *Currently: Forgotten what you enjoy; need to experiment*
  - *Target: Regular creative practice (design, writing, art, or other)*
- [ ] **Deep work on meaningful projects** — Get back to the state where you feel most like yourself
  - *Currently: Deep work exists but not on things you care about*
  - *Target: Deep work + meaningful alignment*
